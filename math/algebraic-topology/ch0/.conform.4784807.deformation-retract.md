# Deformation Retract

## Retraction

**Def** A **deformation retraction** of a space $X$ onto a subspace $A$ is a family of maps $f_t : X \to X$ for $t \in I$ such that $f_0 = 1_X$, $f_1(X) \subset A$, and $f_t|_A = 1_A$ for all $t$. The family $f_t$ is continuous in $t$ and $x$.
